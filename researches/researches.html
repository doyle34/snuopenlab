Advanced Fatigue Analysis Ⅰ

Spectral Fatigue for nonlinear wide band spectrum

  • Loads from wind turbine and transfer function is nonlinear to wind speed
  • Estimation on fatigue damages of floating wind turbine support structure through frequency domain approach
